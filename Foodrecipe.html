Jollof Pasta and Fried Chicken

Pasta has been a favored family food for countless years.So long that many people often question where exactly pasta came from. The origin of pasta can be traceable to both the Chinese and italians.Personnally i love pasta alot, whether white boiled pasta and sauce or jollof pasta.

Let me show you how to prepare Jollof Pasta(Spaghetti) in 5 easy steps

There are many different ways to prepare Jollof Pasta(Spaghetti). This is a dish made with pasta(spaghetti), tomatoes, pepper/onions paste, onions, red bell pepper, scotch bonnet peppers, salt, and spices. I normally like to add some veges(sweetcorn and carrot).

INGREDIENTS

S/N Ingredients Measurements
001 Tomatoes 5 medium sized (chopped in square shapes)
002 red bell pepper Two(chopped or sliced in square shapes)
003 onion 1 bulb(chop in square shapes)
004 scotch bonnet peppers 2
005 groundnut oil 1/2 cup
006 pepper/onions paste 1 sachet
007 Pasta(spaghetti) 1 pack
008 chicken stock 2 cups
009 salt 1 tsp
010 curry powder 1/2 tsp
011 thyme 1/2 tsp
012 Veges(carrot and sweet corn) 2 carrot tuber and 1 mediun can of sweet corn
013 Knorr stock 1 cube
014 Bay leaves 3
015 Water as needed

    Instructions

  1. Slice your tomatoes, red pepper, scotch bonnet peppers and onions in a square shape.
  2. In a medium sized pot, heat your oil on medium-high heat. Once the oil is heated add a pinch of salt,some of your sliced onions and pepper/onions paste. Now stir the paste and leave to fry for a while.
  3. After 10 minutes, turn the heat down to medium, and add the chicken stock. Mix and add your seasonings (salt, curry powder, thyme and the Knorr stock cube). Continue to boil for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the pasta(spaghetti) to the pot. Mix it very well with the sauce. At this point you can add a little water Add the bay leaves, cover the pot, and cook on medium to low heat for 15-30 minutes.
  5. When the liquid has almost dried up add you veges(carrot,onions and sweetcorn) and let it cook for another 5 minutes heat until the liquid has completely dried up. Turn off the heat, mix thoroughly, and your Jollof Pasta(spaghetti)is ready to be eaten!
